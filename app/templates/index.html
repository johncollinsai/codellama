You can switch between assistants at any time. Internal assistants are are fine-tuned GPT models that are used within an organization, such as Falcon and Llama-2. These models are not accessible through a public API service and are used for specific internal purposes. On the other hand, external GPT models are accessed through a public API service, such as ChatGPT, Claude-2, or Bard. These models are designed for general use and can be accessed by anyone with an internet connection.

The main purpose of deriving a distinction between internal and external GPT models is information security. Internal GPT models are used within an organization and are not accessible to the public, which means that they are less vulnerable. External GPT models, on the other hand, are accessible to the public, which makes them more vulnerable, for example to cyber attacks.
